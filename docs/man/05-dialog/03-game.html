The Game Details Dialog

Use this dialog to edit general game information, like player names, event, date, etc.

All fields are optional.

The Event Tab

Event:

Enter here the tournament, or event where the game was played.

Site:

Enter here the location where the game was played.

Date:

Enter here the date when the game was played.
Day and month are optional, it is sufficient to enter a year. You can also enter ?? for unknown parts:

1998/10/14
1998, October 14th
1974/??/??
1974, day and month unknown

Event Date:

Enter here the date when the event was started.

Round:

Enter here the tournament round (if applicable).

Board:

Enter here the board (e.g. for team tournaments).

The Players Tab

Name:

Enter here the white and black player's name.
The format is "Last Name, First Name" by convention, but you are free to use other formats.

ELO:

Enter here the white and black player's ELO rating (if known).
Only positive numbers are accepted in this field.

Title:

Enter here the white and black player'y title (e.g. GM for a grandmaster).

Result:

Select the result of the game. If the result is not known, click *.

The "More" Tab

ECO:

Enter here the ECO opening code.
Note that you can automatically assign ECO codes with Classify ECO .

Opening:

Enter here the Opening name.
Note that you can automaticall assign Opening names with Classify ECO .

Annotator:

Enter here the name of the annotator (if applicable).
